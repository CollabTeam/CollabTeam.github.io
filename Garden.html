Gardening

There are many things to know about gardening:

  • Gardening is fun
  • Gardening is very rewarding
  • Weather and pests can make gardening challenging.

How to start a garden

  1. Locate a sunny spot with good soil
  2. Till the ground to get it ready for planting
  3. Fence the area to keep out animal pests
  4. Purchase seeds or plants that you wish to grow
  5. Space the seeds or plants according to directions
  6. Water as needed
  7. Remove weeds
  8. Reap the rewards!
